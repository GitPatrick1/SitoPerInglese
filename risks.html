Risks of AI

The rise of artificial intelligence brings with it significant risks, particularly in areas such as job automation, security, and privacy. Automation threatens to displace workers in industries like manufacturing, retail, and transportation. Additionally, AI systems can become targets for cyberattacks, with the potential to cause widespread disruption. The misuse of AI-generated content, like deepfakes, also poses a growing security threat. Moreover, there is the risk of bias in AI algorithms, which can perpetuate and even worsen existing inequalities if not carefully monitored.
